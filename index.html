About This Application

Welcome to the Family Relationship Chatbot, an intelligent conversational system that understands and processes family relationships using logic-based inference. This chatbot leverages PROLOG as its inference engine to maintain a knowledge base of family relationships and answer complex queries about them.

🧠 Intelligent Inference

Uses PROLOG's logical reasoning to infer relationships that weren't explicitly stated

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Relationships

Understands complex family structures including siblings, parents, grandparents, aunts, uncles, and more

💬 Natural Conversation

Accepts statements to learn new facts and answers questions about family relationships

What the Chatbot Can Do:

  • Learn from statements: "Alice is the mother of Bob"
  • Answer questions: "Is Alice a grandmother of Charlie?"
  • Infer relationships: Automatically deduce grandfather, sibling, and other relationships
  • Validate feasibility: Detect impossible relationships and contradictions
